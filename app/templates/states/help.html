Instructions

Our system should be used as a tool to track and monitor your beehive.

How to use the SD card:

Use our Home tab to save or open previous notes.

1. Remove the SD card from your beehive.

Use our Your Hive tab to look at each hive variable and their latest update.

2. Attach the SD card to your computer via your adapter/reader.

Use our About tab to learn about each hive variable and what is their normal range.

3. Under our Upload Data tab, click the arrow next to Pick files.

4. Choose the file you wish to upload and press Upload All and Okay.

How to save and open your notes:

1. To save a note, type the file name you wish to save it as and click the save button.

2. To open a note, click the choose file button to browse your files, open your desired file and then click the load file button.
